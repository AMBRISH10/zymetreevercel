OUR STORY

Our team comes with the experience and knowledge

Founded in 2022, Zymetree Tech & Trade began with a strong research background focused on addressing major environmental challenges through biotechnology.

Our continuous innovation led to the development of highly effective Microbial Consortia and biological treatment processes that provide powerful alternatives to conventional chemical methods.

From our early breakthroughs in wastewater treatment to the expansion into specialty chemicals and personal care solutions, our journey has always been fueled by one commitment: creating sustainable products that benefit both industry and the environment.

To become a global leader in eco-friendly biotechnology by redefining how the world reduces waste, reuses resources, and recycles treated outputs.

We aim to create sustainable, science-backed solutions that protect the environment, enhance industrial efficiency, and support a healthier future for generations to come.

Zymetree Tech & Trade is an innovative environmental solutions company dedicated to transforming waste into sustainable value. Established in 2022, we specialize in developing advanced Microbial Consortia and environmentally safe Specialty Chemicals that support efficient water treatment, waste management, and industrial process optimization.

With a deep research-driven foundation, we deliver reliable products for Domestic & Industrial Wastewater Treatment, HVAC Water Systems, RO Plants, Housekeeping, Personal Care, Agro, and Cleaning Chemicals—empowering industries to operate cleaner, smarter, and more sustainably.
