About IMPROVE

IMPROVE is an advanced technology known as Integrated Microbial & Phyto-Remediation Of Various Effluents. This technology is an extended and advanced version of constructed wetland systems that can be used to treat both sewage and industrial effluent water.

IMPROVE technology is an engineered system that uses natural processes involving wetland vegetation, soils, and their associated microbial assemblages to improve water quality. It is designed to take advantage of many of the same processes that occur in natural wetlands, but do so within a more controlled environment.

How It Works

It can be often used in conjunction with other wastewater treatment systems, such as septic tanks and aeration basins. IMPROVE technology works by using a combination of physical, chemical, and biological processes to remove pollutants from wastewater.

Applications

IMPROVE technology is a versatile and effective wastewater treatment technology that can be used to treat a variety of wastewater types and achieve a variety of water quality goals.

STP Water Treatment

Municipal wastewater and Industrial STP wastewater from small towns, villages, and industries producing large amounts of sewage water daily.

Industrial Wastewater

Treatment of industrial wastewater from food processing, paper and pulp, textile mills, and various other industries.

Agricultural & Mining

Treatment of agricultural runoff wastewater, mining wastewater, and other water sources like storm water.
