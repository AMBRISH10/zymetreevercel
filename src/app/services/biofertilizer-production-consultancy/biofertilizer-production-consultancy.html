Application Method

Foliar Spray

Dilute 2-5 ml FAA per litre of water. Spray during early morning or evening for best results.

Soil Drench

Soil Conditioner

Soil conditioner is any material added to soil to improve its physical properties, making it more suitable for plant growth. Unlike fertilizers which supply nutrients, soil conditioners enhance the soil's structure, aeration, water retention and overall health.

Improves Soil Structure

Helps sandy soils hold water and clay soils drain better

Enhances Aeration

Promotes better root growth through improved soil aeration

Water Retention

Boosts water-holding capacity for drought resilience

Reduces Compaction

Prevents soil compaction and maintains soil health

Microbial Activity

Promotes beneficial microbial activity in soil

Nutrient Availability

Increases nutrient availability and retention
