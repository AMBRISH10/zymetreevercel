At Zymetree, we are committed to delivering effective wastewater treatment solutions that protect the environment and ensure regulatory compliance. Our specialized chemicals are designed for STPs, ETPs, septic tanks, ponds, and industrial wastewater systems, efficiently removing suspended solids, organic matter, pathogens, and complex contaminants. This ensures treated effluent consistently meets local and international standards, helping industries reduce risks and maintain compliance with confidence.

Our scientifically formulated solutions for coagulation, flocculation, pH adjustment, precipitation, and biological enhancement make wastewater treatment more efficient and predictable. The result is cleaner, clearer water, improved sludge settling, lower costs, and reliable system performance, establishing Zymetree as a trusted partner in sustainable wastewater management.
