Why Choose Biological Solutions?

Unlike chemical fertilizers that supply nutrients directly, biofertilizers work by biological processes, helping plants absorb nutrients more efficiently and improving soil fertility over time.

They are especially important for organic farming, integrated nutrient management and restoration of degraded soils.
