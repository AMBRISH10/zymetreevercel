Established in 2022, Zymetree Tech & Trade emerged from a strong research foundation focused on developing effective microbial consortia. Our mission centers on reducing waste in all forms while maximizing the reuse and recycling of biological resources.

Through continuous research and development, we've created environmentally beneficial microbial solutions for biological treatment of domestic and industrial wastewater. Our comprehensive product portfolio includes specialty chemicals for water treatment in HVAC systems, chiller and cooling towers, RO plants, and housekeeping applications.
